    About Me:

    I grew up believing that I wanted to be a mechanical engineer, and took courses in highschool that introduced me to the field of engineering. However, upon completing my Neverwinter Nights Toolset Capstone project (see above), I realized my passion for Game Design and Computer Science. Though I made this brekathrough, I still enjoyed many of the physical aspects of engineering, and found my place at The Ohio State University through their Computer Science & Engineering major, which even had a Game Design Specialization. Finally, I plan to minor in physics in order to enhance my real-world simulation skills as well as take some Quantum Computing courses offered at The Ohio State University.
